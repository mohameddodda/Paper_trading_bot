# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and maintainers, pledge to make participation in **Paper Trading Bot** a **harassment-free experience** for everyone — regardless of age, background, identity, or experience level.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and educational community.

—

## Our Standards

Examples of **behavior that contributes** to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of **unacceptable behavior**:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Spam, scams, or promotion of real-money trading bots
- **Any attempt to introduce real trading, API keys, or financial risk**
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others’ private information without consent

> **This project is simulation-only. No real money. No real trading.**

—

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.  
They have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove, edit, or reject comments, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for unacceptable behavior

—

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub repository, Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions)
- Public communications representing the project (e.g., social media, talks)

—

—

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction  
**When**: Minor or first-time violation  
**Action**: Private, kind correction with clear guidance

### 2. Warning  
**When**: Repeated or intentional minor violations  
**Action**: Formal warning with consequences outlined

### 3. Temporary Ban  
**When**: Serious or repeated violations  
**Action**: Temporary removal from project spaces

### 4. Permanent Ban  
**When**: Severe, dangerous, or repeated harmful behavior  
**Action**: Permanent exclusion from all project interaction
