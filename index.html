Paper Trading Bot

A risk-free simulation of an AI-powered crypto trading bot, designed to help you practice strategies before using real money.

Currently running on iOS via Pythonista 3, this bot monitors live prices from Crypto.com and uses DeepSeek AI (via OpenRouter) to suggest BUY, SELL, or HOLD signals.

Simulation Only – No Real Funds
Important: This is paper trading only. No real money is involved. Use it to test logic, timing, and AI accuracy.

Next Step: A PC version is in development to allow faster testing, backtesting, and strategy optimization before any live deployment.

Think of this as your training ground — build confidence, refine rules, and prove the system works.
